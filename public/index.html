Community Overview

Welcome to Watkins MHC, your gateway to clean, safe, and affordable housing in Watkins, MN 55389. Our all-ages community features approximately 20 home sites in the heart of Meeker County lake country, offering small-town living with easy access to St. Cloud's regional services just 25-35 minutes away. With lot rent at only $325 per month, you'll enjoy simple, quiet community living that emphasizes affordability, individual yards, and basic services.
